RASR

What is RASR?

We wanted to create an environment where players can express themselves through the art of map editing, while being able to explore and see what other users have created. To build this game, we integrated a wide range of technologies, from sophisticated frameworks such as Angular and Phaser.io, to cutting edge real-time engines like Socket.io

How it works

Players build and customize the world as they see fit. Along the way, they explore this open-source world, fight creatures, gain rewards, and interact with other players and their surroundings. With the arrow keys, the player is able to move through their surroundings. You can destroy enemies by using fireballs with space. By clicking on the Earth icon in the bottom-left corner, the player can create and new maps.
